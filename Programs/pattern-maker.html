Click on the Canvas (or most the controls) to regenerate the pattern.

The number associated with a color determines how frequently it is picked.

Choose from 1 to 20 to pick the proportion (like 1 part red, 2 parts gold, 1 part really dark gray).

Choosing 0 will keep the color in the list, but it will not show up.

Choosing -1 will remove the color from the list.
